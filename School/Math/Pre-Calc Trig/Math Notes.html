Law of Cosines

If $ABC$ is a triangle with sides $a$, $b$, and $c$, then

$$a^2=b^2-2bc\cos{A}$$

$$\cos{A}=\frac{b^2+c^2-a^2}{2bc}$$

Law of Sines

to find angles:

$$\frac{\sin{A}}{a}=\frac{\sin{B}}{b}=\frac{\sin{C}}{c}$$

to find side lengths:

$$\frac{a}{\sin{A}}=\frac{b}{\sin{B}}=\frac{c}{\sin{C}}$$
