## Part 1 - Solving a Triangle with Three Sides (SSS)
### Law of Cosines
If $ABC$ is a triangle with sides $a$, $b$, and $c$, then
$$a^2=b^2-2bc\cos{A}$$
$$\cos{A}=\frac{b^2+c^2-a^2}{2bc}$$
